Weiyan Shi

Hi 👋. I'm currently a Ph.D. student at Context-Aware Interaction Lab, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), under the supervision of Prof. Kenny Choo.

I received my Master’s degree supervised by Prof. Daqing Zhang, IEEE Fellow from Peking University (PKU) in 2024. Before PKU, I earned a Bachelor’s degree from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2021. From 2023 to 2024, I worked as a research assistant at Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR.

I'm interested in Human-Computer Interaction and Ubiquitous Computing. Most of my research focuses on sensing human behaviors through ubiquitous data sources. Recently, I have been exploring the use of large language models (LLMs) to assist in sensing and interpreting human interaction behaviors.
